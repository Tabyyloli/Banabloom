About Us



BANABLOOM is one the newly discovered ways of making the famous delicacy safer and healthier. It contains high nutritional value and are high in dietary fiber as well as some biologically active compounds such as vitamin C, tannin, myoinositol phosphates, and alpha tocopherol. High dietary fiber intake is associated to significantly lower prevalence rates of peripheral vascular disease, coronary heart disease, and stroke.
